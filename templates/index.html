Welcome to our Stock Predictor!

This is a website that will show stock predictions for the next thirty days for 10 chosen stocks.

There is a page for each model's prediction; both are similar with minor differences in the model itself and variables used, but serve the same purpose.

If you click on each stock graph, you can look at the previous 30 days of predictions to see how accurate the previous forecasts are.

  • Utilizes Tensorflow and Keras libraries to construct models
  • Uses LSTM Models on both stock returns and stock prices to forecast results
  • Recursively calls functions on previous predictions for future predictions
